# Audio File Instructions

## Required Audio File

Place your wedding card opening sound effect in this directory:

**Filename:** `open-card.mp3`
**Path:** `/public/audio/open-card.mp3`

## Recommended Sound Effects

For the best user experience, use a gentle, elegant sound effect such as:

1. **Paper rustling** - Soft paper opening sound
2. **Gentle chime** - Subtle bell or chime sound
3. **Magic sparkle** - Light, magical twinkling sound
4. **Soft swoosh** - Gentle whoosh effect

## Audio Specifications

- **Format:** MP3 (recommended) or WAV
- **Duration:** 0.5 - 2 seconds
- **Volume:** Medium-low (the component sets volume to 0.5/50%)
- **File size:** Keep under 100KB for fast loading

## Where to Find Free Sound Effects

1. **Pixabay** - https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/
2. **Freesound** - https://freesound.org/
3. **Zapsplat** - https://www.zapsplat.com/
4. **Mixkit** - https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/

## Search Keywords

When looking for sound effects, search for:
- "paper open"
- "card flip"
- "gentle chime"
- "magic sparkle"
- "soft whoosh"
- "elegant transition"

## Optional: Disable Sound

If you don't want sound effects, the component will gracefully handle missing audio files. The animation will still work perfectly without sound.
